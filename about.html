Atlantic Vocational Training Institute

We are a private, non-profit, technical and vocational training institution that is providing intensive and cost effective TVET. Atlantic Vocational Training Institute is aimed at providing comprehensive career-focused education and training. Our programs emphasis is on real-world education for real-world jobs and help you acquire hands-on skills you need to gain employment. We offer more than 16 programs - diploma and certificate options - that you can complete in three, six and twelve months respectively. Programs in the fastest growing fields, plus emerging industries and technologies, where employers need a highly skilled workforce and our graduates are in demand. The institute is located In Doe community, Liberia a country on Africa's west coast. Our Mission & Our Objectives

Our Mission is to promote and foster individuals and organizational effectiveness by developing and offering an array of innovative and diverse programs in support of the institution's commitment to employee development, partnerships, and organizational enrichment. We do this by improving their employment prospects, wellbeing and quality of life through improved skill sets. Our programs are enhanced by developing and maintaining partnerships with business, industry, and the community. Our faculties are dedicated to teaching, advising, and training our students. To prepare students for further vocational and technical education and lifelong learning.

To produce craftsmen, technicians and other skilled manpower to meet the demands of industry, agriculture, commerce and other technical services as well as the teaching of technical and vocational subjects Train youth in demand driven technical and vocational skills as a means of enhancing youth employability. Stimulate intellectual and technical growth of students in order to make them productive members of the community; Provide specialized training initial or continuing. To satisfy the continuously changing needs of the labor market. To enhance the trainees who have completed the highest level of secondary education.
